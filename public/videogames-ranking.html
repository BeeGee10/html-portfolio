The Best Videogames According to BeeGee

My top 3 videogames of all-time.

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim

Skyrim is a classic. Even after all these years, the visuals still impress me. The world is massive, atmospheric, and immersive. For a game released in 2011, it was way ahead of its time and still holds up today.

Phasmophobia

I’m a big fan of horror games, and Phasmophobia hits the sweet spot with its mix of jump scares and puzzle-solving. Investigating haunted locations with friends keeps things tense and fun, and no two games ever feel the same.

Super Mega Baseball 4

As a baseball fan, I love this one. It’s got solid gameplay and surprisingly deep mechanics, but what really makes it stand out are the hilarious and over-the-top player names. It doesn’t take itself too seriously, which makes it a blast to play.
